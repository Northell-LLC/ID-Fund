Deal Summary
Credit Sesame is a data analytics and artificial intelligence company that has already helped 15MM+ users improve their credit scores, increase their approval odds, lower the cost of credit, and save money.
They are funded by leading venture capital firms and strategic investors, including Menlo Ventures, Inventus Capital, Globespan Capital, IA Capital Groups, Symantec, Capital One Ventures, and Stanford University, among others. Credit Sesame closed a $30MM Series F round in late 2019 at a $355MM post-money valuation. The ID Fund has shares at $12.60 per share or a $375MM valuation.

Credit Sesame CEO and Founder Adrian Nazari is considered one of the top professionals in the credit industry. Prior to starting Credit Sesame, Nazari started two credit companies that sold to publicly traded companies (LPL Financial and Computer Associates).
Nazari started Credit Sesame in 2010 with a simple mission: give people the tools they need to fix their credit and achieve financial freedom. This created a credit fixing engine, like nothing else out there, that currently has millions of customers.
At a time when the digital banking industry was worth nearly $375B, Credit Sesame realized the process for signing up for their credit business was very similar to opening a digital bank account. Within a year of launching Credit Sesame's digital banking unit, the Company had over 1 million customers–– a feat that took Chime Digital Bank over 5 years to achieve.
Due to the simplicity of converting credit customers to banking customers, Credit Sesame is able to acquire digital banking customers at zero user acquisition cost. At the same time, Credit Sesame is the only company on Earth offering their banking customers cash for improving their credit. In an industry where competition is everywhere, Credit Sesame has an 85% annual retention rate.
The Company is currently adding nearly 100,000 digital banking accounts a month without advertising spend. With their recent capital raise and an investment into marketing, the Company believes they can add 500,000 accounts per month.
To put that in perspective, Current Digital Bank is adding 100,000 accounts a month and recently went from a $750MM valuation to a $2.2 billion valuation in just 5 months.
Credit Sesame raised money at a $344MM valuation in the fall of 2019 and recently closed out a strategic pre-IPO round led by a $20MM investment from TransUnion at a $701MM valuation.
Investors in Credit Sesame include: Menlo Ventures, TransUnion, Capital One Ventures, Stanford University Symantec, LifeLock, Inventus Capital, Camp One Ventures, Globespan Capital, and IA Capital Groups among others.
Credit Sesame is reportedly working with Goldman Sachs on a $2B+ IPO this year. We have shares at $25.20, or a $917MM valuation.
